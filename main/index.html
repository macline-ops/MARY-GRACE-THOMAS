About Mary Grace

Mary Grace Thomas is an anointed worship leader and Christian artist with a passion for ushering people into God's presence through music. Her journey in worship ministry began in her local church as a teenager.

With a unique blend of contemporary worship and deep spiritual themes, Mary Grace's music reflects her intimate walk with God and her calling to see lives transformed through worship.

Her songs have become worship anthems in churches worldwide, touching hearts and drawing people closer to Christ through powerful lyrics and uplifting melodies.
